welcome to co.up community space

We are a community space located in Berlin-Kreuzberg, providing event and workshop spaces for the Berlin tech and creative community. We also offer coworking!

co.up provides event and workshop spaces for events in the local tech and creative scene. Most of our events are focused on software development, creative programming and digital design, but people from all fields are welcome.

During the day, we offer coworking on a fixed and flex-desk basis in our well-lit open space, as well as meeting room rentals. Simply drop us a line at hello at co-up dot de!

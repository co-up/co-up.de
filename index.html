Welcome to co.up Community Space

We are a community space located in Berlin-Kreuzberg, providing event and workshop spaces for the Berlin tech and creative community. As of November 2021, we also offer coworking again!

co.up provides event and workshop spaces for events in the local tech and creative scene. Most of our events are focused on software development, creative programming and digital design, but people from all fields are welcome.

During the day, we offer coworking on a flex-desk basis in our quiet and well-lit open space, as well as meeting room rentals. Simply drop us a line at hello at co-up dot de!

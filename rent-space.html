Host your project in a living piece of Berlin’s tech history

Need space for a meeting, a workshop, a presentation, a teambuilding session, or just a few calls? You can book our meeting room at co.up, a community space with a purpose in the heart of Kreuzberg.

co.up's story goes back to the early days of Berlin's tech scene, when it was one of the first coworking spaces forging a new way to work by bridging the gap between professionals from the creative and tech worlds; today it's also a community space that continues a tradition of supporting and serving underrepresented communities in tech. Our meeting room seats 10 people and is available for external bookings, which helps us to continue our mission to make the tech industry more representative. Contact hello@co-up.de to check our availability!

What we offer:

  • A 42sqm meeting room with 1 table seating 12 people and 1 table seating 4 people (additional chairs available), a whiteboard, a flipchart, and a TV screen (HDMI).
  • Full use of kitchen including a coffee maker, microwave, water heater, and dishwasher.
  • High-speed internet.
  • Choose from a wide selection of 2€ drinks, including mate soda, cola and fruit sodas
  • All-gender restrooms (note: unfortunately these are not wheelchair-accessible. The nearest ones that are can be found at neighboring Mittelpunkt Bibliothek, Möbel Olfe and Südblock).
  • Convenient elevator access.
  • Outside catering is welcome.

Pricing

Meeting Room - special summertime offer!

  • up to 8 people: 7€/hour, 35€/day (excl. VAT/MwSt)
  • 9-20 people: 20€/hour, 90€/day (excl. VAT/MwSt)

We also offer coworking!
